describe

NoSQL East, a conference of non-relational data stores, aims to present the experiences of different companies using so-called "nosql" solutions in production. The reasons for using these new systems are varied, but often involve scale that relational solutions cannot achieve.
Join us in Atlanta as we discuss "Big Data" and the systems that are making short work of mammoth data sets.
